Background:

NFTScoring is the place for you to discover, analyze and trade NFTs, giving you the superpowers to understand the NFT market in any given moment, make the best decisions, and take faster actions. We designed a mobile interface to help experienced NFT collectors more easily find investment data. Our interface helps individuals with significant NFT experience make better decisions.

Interactive Prototype and Feedback

We built a figma mockup of the wireframes (shown right) to bring to the studio critique. Overall, feedback was mostly positive with the main suggestions being differentiate the buy and sell pages, add an "add favorites" function, give users more flexibility to edit their profile, and our overall dark mode was hard to read. With this feedback, we redesigned the interface with more attractive colors to drown out the black background. On top of this we added an option to favorite a particular NFT, and gave users a better experience when viewing their own profile. The finished result can be found below.

Observations and Responses

1. All users:

- Had to swipe more than one time to get the machine to register their cash
- Used flex points rather than credit card or cash
- Seemed to struggle when they bent down to swipe their card
- Were confused as to when they could select a snack
- Opted for a high-sugar snack

2. Some users:

- Had a skittles packet stuck and had to shake the vending machine to dispense their snack
- Had to press the snack code buttons multiple times to get their desired item
